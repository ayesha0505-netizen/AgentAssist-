# Refund and Return Policy

## 30-Day Return Window
HelpFlow AI and our retail partners offer a comprehensive 30-day return window from the date your item is delivered. If 30 days have passed since your delivery date, we unfortunately cannot offer a full refund or exchange unless covered under our separate Warranty Policy.

## Eligibility Criteria for Refunds
To be eligible for a return and full refund:
1. The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the exact same condition that you received it.
2. All original tags, accessories, and instruction manuals must be included in the return package.
3. Proof of purchase or a valid Order ID (`ORDxxxx`) must be provided.

## Restocking Fees & Exceptions
- **Unopened Items**: 100% full refund with no restocking fee.
- **Opened but Unused Items**: Subject to a 10% restocking fee to cover inspection and re-packaging.
- **Damaged or Defective upon Arrival**: 100% full refund or free replacement shipping. If your item arrived defective, please ask our AI assistant to create a support ticket immediately.
- **Non-Returnable Items**: Gift cards, downloadable software products, and personalized custom items cannot be returned for a refund.

## Refund Processing Time
Once your return package is received at our inspection facility, we will process your item within 2 business days. If approved, the refund will automatically be credited back to your original payment method (Credit Card, Debit Card, or Bank Transfer) within 5 to 7 business days depending on your financial institution.
