# Limited Manufacturer Warranty Policy

## Standard 1-Year Limited Warranty
All electronic products sold through AgentAssist AI include a **1-Year Limited Manufacturer Warranty** from the original date of purchase. This warranty covers defects in materials and quality under normal customer use.

## What is Covered Under Warranty?
- Internal hardware failure not caused by external impact or physical damage.
- Battery degradation exceeding 40% loss of maximum charge capacity within the first 12 months.
- Manufacturing faults in ports, buttons, screens, and internal processors.

## What is NOT Covered (Exclusions)?
- Accidental drops, liquid spills, water submersion, or physical cracks caused by the customer.
- Unauthorized repairs, third-party modification, or opening the sealed chassis.
- Cosmetic wear and tear, including surface scratches, scuffs, or paint peeling from daily usage.
- Damage caused by power surges, lightning strikes, or extreme temperature exposure.

## How to Submit a Warranty Claim
1. Ask our AI assistant to check your product details and create a Warranty Claim Ticket. Be sure to provide your Order ID (`ORDxxxx`) and a brief description of the hardware failure.
2. Once your ticket (`TCK-xxx`) is generated, our technical support team will review the issue within 24 hours.
3. If pre-approved, we will email you a prepaid return shipping label. Once received at our repair center, we will repair or replace the defective unit within 5 to 7 business days at no charge.
