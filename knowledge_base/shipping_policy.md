# Shipping and Delivery Policy

## Domestic Shipping Options & Rates
We ship across all 50 states using top-tier logistics partners including FedEx, UPS, and USPS.
- **Standard Shipping (5–7 Business Days)**: Free on all orders over $50.00. For orders under $50.00, a flat shipping fee of $4.99 applies.
- **Expedited Shipping (2–3 Business Days)**: Flat rate of $12.99 nationwide.
- **Priority Overnight Shipping (1 Business Day)**: Flat rate of $24.99 for orders placed before 2:00 PM EST.

## Order Processing & Handling Time
All orders are processed within 1 to 2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or national holidays will begin processing on the following business day. Once your package transitions from `PROCESSING` to `SHIPPED`, a tracking number will automatically be generated.

## Shipping Delays and Lost Packages
While 98% of orders arrive on or before their `expected_delivery` date, unforeseen weather events or carrier delays can occasionally occur.
- If your order status is marked as `DELAYED`, please allow an additional 48 hours beyond the expected delivery date.
- If your package has not arrived within 5 business days after the estimated delivery date, please ask our AI assistant to open an urgent shipping investigation ticket (`TCK-xxx`). Our fulfillment team will dispatch a replacement or issue a full refund within 24 hours.

## International Shipping
We currently ship to over 30 countries across North America, Europe, and Asia. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on package weight and destination. Customers are responsible for any customs duties, VAT, or import taxes levied by their local customs authorities.
