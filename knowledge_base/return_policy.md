# Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) & Return Procedures

## 1. Overview of Return Procedures
HelpFlow AI is committed to ensuring a seamless return experience for all customers. Whether you ordered the wrong hardware specification or changed your mind after delivery, our automated RMA processing allows you to initiate return requests directly through our autonomous support engine or with our support engineering team.

## 2. Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) Process
To initiate a return and receive a prepaid shipping label, customers must obtain a valid Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number:
1. **Automated Request**: Ask the HelpFlow AI assistant: *"I need to initiate a return for my order #ORDxxxx"* or *"Generate a return shipping label for my item."*
2. **System Verification**: The AI assistant verifies your order status, confirms delivery date within the 30-day window, and generates an RMA ticket ID (`TCK-xxx`).
3. **Prepaid Label Issuance**: Once authorized, an electronic prepaid return shipping label (FedEx or UPS) along with detailed return packing instructions will be emailed to your registered account address immediately.

## 3. Packaging & Shipment Guidelines
To ensure safe transit and full refund or exchange eligibility, please adhere to the following packaging guidelines:
- **Original Box Required**: Pack the item securely inside its original manufacturer box with all molded foam inserts, protective covers, cables, and user manuals intact.
- **Outer Protective Shipping Box**: Place the product box inside a sturdy corrugated outer shipping box with adequate cushioning material (bubble wrap or packing peanuts) to prevent movement during transit. Do NOT attach shipping labels directly to the product retail box.
- **RMA Label Placement**: Affix the provided prepaid FedEx/UPS return label securely on the outside of the shipping box over any previous labels or barcodes.
- **RMA Slip Insertion**: Print and place a copy of your RMA confirmation email or ticket ID slip inside the top of the outer shipping box before sealing.

## 4. Return Inspection & Verification Timelines
- **Arrival at Facility**: Once dropped off at an authorized carrier depot, return tracking status can be monitored live via the HelpFlow AI chat interface.
- **Technical Inspection**: Upon arrival at our primary hardware inspection center in San Jose, CA, our quality assurance engineering team completes a thorough physical and diagnostic evaluation within 1 to 2 business days.
- **Status Notification**: You will receive an automated email notification and status update once the return item passes inspection.

## 5. Direct Exchanges & Replacement Shipping
If you requested a hardware exchange (e.g. exchanging a laptop model or switching keyboard switch types) rather than a refund:
- **Advanced Replacement Option**: For critical business operations, customers can request an Advanced Replacement by asking our support team. A replacement unit is dispatched immediately while placing a temporary hold on your payment card until the original unit arrives.
- **Standard Exchange**: Once your returned item passes technical inspection at our facility, the replacement item is immediately shipped via Expedited 2-Day Shipping at zero additional cost to you.

## 6. International & Cross-Border Returns
- For orders shipped outside the United States, return authorization can still be initiated via HelpFlow AI.
- International customers are provided with commercial invoice documentation to attach to the return pouch for customs clearance.
- Unless the return is due to a manufacturer defect or shipping error by HelpFlow AI, international return shipping costs and customs brokerage fees will be deducted from the final refund credit.

## 7. Return Exclusions & Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be accepted for RMA return processing:
- Custom-built servers, built-to-order workstation clusters, or laser-engraved hardware.
- Opened consumable items such as thermal paste, cleaning kits, or battery packs once protective seals are broken.
- Downloadable software licenses, digital subscription keys, or cloud credits once activated.
