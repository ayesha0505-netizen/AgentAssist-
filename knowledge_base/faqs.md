# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## How do I create an account?
To create an account with AgentAssist AI, click the "Register" or "Login" tab on the top navigation bar. Enter your full name, email address, and a secure password. Once registered, you will have immediate access to customer support chat, order history, and support ticket tracking.

## How can I track or check the status of my order?
You can easily track your order directly through our AI support chat! Simply ask the AI assistant: *"Where is my order #ORD1005?"* or *"Check status for order ORD1008."* Our Agentic AI will query our live backend systems and provide your real-time status and expected delivery date instantly.

## What should I do if my order is delayed?
If your order status shows as `DELAYED`, our AI assistant can immediately create a priority support ticket for you. Simply let the assistant know you would like to open a ticket regarding your delayed shipment, and it will generate a unique tracking ID (`TCK-xxx`) that our logistics team monitors 24/7.

## How do I contact a live human support agent?
While our Agentic AI resolves over 90% of customer inquiries instantly, you can request human escalation at any time. If our AI cannot resolve your issue, or if you ask for a human supervisor, your support ticket will be flagged for review by our live escalation team during regular business hours (Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM EST).

## Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
Orders can only be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement, provided the status is still listed as `PROCESSING`. Once an order transitions to `SHIPPED`, it cannot be cancelled or modified, and you will need to follow our standard refund and return process after delivery.
